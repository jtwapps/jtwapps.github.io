Welcome to Wild Violets!

Wild Violets is our streamlined a la carte wedding flower ordering option.

It is intended for flower-loving but busy couples who only need to order bouquets, wearable flowers, or centerpieces from us and who don’t need full-service floral design.

All of our designs are organic, abundant, and inspired by the season, with an emphasis on color and unique blooms.

The Wild Violets option is designed to make ordering beautiful wedding flowers quick work: this means fewer phone calls, emails, and decisions for you.

Ready to get started?

If you're ready to get started, use the form below to tell us more about your wedding, choose from our favorite seasonal color palettes, and select the estimated quantities of floral elements you need, to generate an instant floral design proposal.

When you're happy with your estimate and you're ready to take the next step, submit your proposal to us so we can confirm our availability, and then you can immediately save your date with us with a contract and 25% deposit.

Please note we can accommodate orders on Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays in the months of March-November only at this time.

Ordering 2-6 months out from your wedding day is recommended.

After confirming your order with us and saving your date by booking, you'll connect with us in a Details Call to review your flowers in more depth before the big day.

Orders must be finalized and paid 21 days out from the wedding.

Please be prepared to complete this form in one session. Your progress will be lost if you use your browser's "Back" button, leave our site, or refresh the page.
